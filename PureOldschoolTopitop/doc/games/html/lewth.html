Lewthwaite's Game

Game Play
The Board:
5x5 board

The Pieces:
Black and White pieces. All 24 pieces are placed on the board alternating black and white. All spaces are filled except the middle.

Rules:
To move: Players move either vertically or horizontally into an open spot.
To win: Stalemate the opponent.

Players alternate turns either moving vertically or horizontally into an open spot. The first person to stalemate the other player (they cannot move) wins the game.

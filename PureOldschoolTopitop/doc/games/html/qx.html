Quick Cross

History

In 1997,Quick Cross was made by the Great American Trading Company.

Game Play

The Board:
A 4x4 board with a cross shape in each space.

The Pieces:
Each player has 8 pieces each.

Rules:
To move: Players either place a piece or switch the direction of the piece in subsequent moves.
To win: The first person to get four in a row vertically, horizontally or diagonally wins.

The first player places one of the sixteen pieces on the board in any direction. The next player then either adds a piece or switches the direction of a piece on the board. Play continues until one person gets four in a row horizontally, vertically or diagonally.

Variants:
Misere: To force your opponent into getting four in a row either vertically, horizontally or diagonally.
Corners and Diagonals: The player to score all four corners or diagonals wins.

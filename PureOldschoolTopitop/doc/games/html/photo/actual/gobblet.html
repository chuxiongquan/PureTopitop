Gobblet

Game Play

The Board:
3x3 board

The Pieces:
Each player has two large and two small pieces.

Rules:
To move: Players may either place a piece or move a piece.
To win: To get three pieces in a row.

The players each have two large and two small pieces on a 3x3 board. On each turn a player can either place a piece or move a piece. In moving a piece, a large piece can go over a smaller piece, but not vice versa. The game ends when one player has three in a row of his/her pieces.

Variants:
Misere: To force your opponent into getting three of his pieces in a row.

Game Play

The Board:
The standard Tic Tac Tier! board is a 3x3 grid of squares.
Starting position: Nothing on the board.

The Pieces:
There are two types of pieces, X's (player 1) and O's (player 2).

Rules:
To move: Each player takes turns making one valid move.
Valid Moves:
Choose an empty position to place your piece in.
To win: Be the first player to get three of your pieces (either X or O) in a row, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally. 3-in-a-row WINS.

Whoever is X goes first. The players alternate making legal moves.

Variants:
Misere: Force the opponent to get three of their pieces (either X or O) in a row, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.

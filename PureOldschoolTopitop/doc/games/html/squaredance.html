Square Dance

Game Play

The Board:
3x3

The Pieces:
Black or white pieces in an up or down orientation.

Rules:
To move: Place your pieces on an open spot.
To win: Form a square with four pieces sharing the same attributes.

Players alternate turns placing a piece on the board either up or down until one person forms a square sharing either the player's color or the configuration of up or down.
